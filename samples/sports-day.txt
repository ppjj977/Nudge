Subject: Sports Day tomorrow - reminders and information

Dear Parents and Carers,

Ahead of Sports Day tomorrow, we would like to share some important information
and reminders to help the day run smoothly.

- The school gates will close at 8:50am. To access the school field for Sports
  Day, please use the gates by the bike shed, as these will be the only gates
  open during the day.
- Children should arrive at school at the usual registration time of 8:40am.
- The school office will be closed from 9:15am. If you need to contact us,
  please use the main school telephone number, which will be monitored
  throughout the day.
- Children should wear PE kit. Reception children can wear shorts, a t-shirt
  and trainers.
- Children should bring in a named water bottle.
- There will be no school lunches available tomorrow, as families are invited
  to join us for a picnic lunch with their children. If you are unable to
  attend, please ensure your child brings a packed lunch.
- Adult toilet facilities will be available. Please follow the signage.

We are very much looking forward to welcoming you tomorrow and hope the weather
is kind to us. It may be worth bringing a waterproof coat or umbrella just in
case of the occasional shower.
